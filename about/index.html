Our story

Flowers you can
actually taste

Blooming began with a simple idea, that a celebration cake should be as beautiful as the moment it marks. We specialise in Korean rice cakes and bean paste flowers, each one piped petal by petal in our Auckland, New Zealand studio.

From 100 day blessings to birthdays and weddings, every cake is made to order with natural ingredients and a lot of patience, never from a mould, never from a fridge.

01

Made fresh, to order

Every cake is baked and decorated only after you order, never sitting in a fridge, always at its best for your day.

Honest, high-quality ingredients

Real butter, fresh cream and natural bean paste. No artificial shortcuts, just flavour you can taste and trust.

Designed around your moment
