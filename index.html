Our story

Flowers you can
actually taste

Blooming began with a simple idea — that a celebration cake should be as beautiful as the moment it marks. We specialise in Korean rice cakes and bean-paste flowers, each one piped petal by petal in our Auckland studio.

From 100-day blessings to birthdays and weddings, every cake is made to order with natural ingredients and a lot of patience — never from a mould, never from a fridge.

— Blooming, Auckland
